What are the three stages in the problem solving process?
Answer: Understand the problem, plan how to solve it and divide it into small subproblems.

Why is it important to clearly understand the problem first?
Answer: You can't solve something that you barely understand.

What can you do to help get a clearer understanding of the problem?
Answer: Plan how to solve it.

What are some of the things you should do in the planning stage of the problem solving process?
Answer: Write the plan out on a paper or try to explain to someone what you understood.

What is an algorithm?
Answer: An instruction that solves something.

What is pseudocode?
Answer: An english instruction that intends to solve something.

What are the advantages of breaking a problem down and solving the smaller problems?
Answer: It makes the problem easier.
